# DevLog - Product Requirements

## Problem
Developers working across multiple repos lose track of what they did. Standup prep, changelog writing, and weekly summaries are manual and tedious.

## Solution
A TUI tool that scans git repos, aggregates commits, and uses AI to generate human-readable summaries.

## Target User
Individual developers and small team leads who work across 3+ repos.

## Success Metrics
- Time to generate weekly summary: <30 seconds (vs 15+ minutes manual)
- Supports 20+ repos without performance degradation
- AI summaries rated "useful" by user >80% of the time

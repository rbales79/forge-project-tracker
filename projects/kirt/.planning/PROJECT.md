# KIRT — Project Definition

## What

KIRT (Knowledge In Real Time) is the AI-powered intelligence layer for strategic account management. It sits on top of Forge Foundation v3.0 and serves anyone who needs account intelligence — from Enterprise Architects running the full Strategic Account Framework (SAF) to AEs who need to prep for a meeting in 10 minutes.

KIRT generates Account Briefs, Engagement Prep packages, Meeting Summaries, Client Deliverables, and Cross-Sell Recommendations from the organization's own data: SharePoint documents, Salesforce records, meeting notes, and public data (SEC filings, earnings, news).

**KIRT is not a CRM replacement.** SF stays as system of record. KIRT adds unstructured analysis, semantic search, AI generation, and pattern intelligence.

## Why

**The problem:** Strategic account preparation takes 2-8 hours per account, per engagement. Intelligence is scattered across SharePoint, email, Salesforce, and tribal knowledge. There is no unified view of what we know about an account, and most of that knowledge walks out the door when people leave.

**The value:** 80%+ reduction in document preparation time. 50-70% improvement in account intelligence quality. A non-technical stakeholder can watch a 10-minute demo and understand the value without explanation.

## Who

| User | Mode | How They Use KIRT |
|------|------|-------------------|
| Enterprise Architects / Strategic Account Leads | Full SAF | Sequential SAF phases, scoring pipeline, full deliverable types, SAF progress tracking |
| AEs / Solution Specialists | On-Demand Intelligence | Upload notes, generate briefs, search, prep for meetings. No framework required. |
| Sales Leadership / Delivery/PM | Consumer | Search, browse, read. Consume intelligence generated by others. |

## Constraints

- **Foundation v3.0 dependency.** 13 REST API endpoints, Weaviate, Iceberg, Redis Streams. No v4 features.
- **Single-tenant V1.** Internal Pellera deployment only.
- **Web app only.** Responsive (mobile: read flows). Desktop: generation + admin.
- **LLM: multi-provider abstraction.** Gemini, Azure OpenAI, AWS Bedrock. No provider-specific code.
- **SP is document source-of-truth.** KIRT writes new deliverable files, never edits source files.
- **Permission inheritance is strict.** If you can't see it in SP, you can't see it in KIRT.
- **MSA opt-out is absolute.** `ai_processing_allowed: false` → keyword search only. No LLM ever.
- **Offerings catalog and discovery question taxonomy must be created before demo.** Content dependency — not code.

## Ecosystem

- **Forge Foundation v3.0** — upstream dependency. Foundation provides search, storage, ingestion, permissions.
- **SharePoint** — document source-of-truth. KIRT reads from Foundation (which ingests from SP). KIRT writes deliverables back to designated SP output folders.
- **Salesforce** — CRM source. V1: CSV exports ingested into Foundation. V4+: direct API.
